    Summary of the 3 plots

  • Histogram:

    The distribution of BorrowerAPR is approximately normal, with a peak around 0.2. The majority of loans have an APR between 0.1 and 0.3. There are a few loans with APRs as high as 0.5.

  • Kernel density plot:

    Confirms the findings from the histogram, showing a normal-like distribution with a peak around 0.2.

  • Box plot:

    The median APR is around 0.2, with the majority of loans falling between 0.1 and 0.3. There are a few loans with APRs above 0.4, which are considered outliers. The box spans the IQR, or the middle 50% of the data, with the whiskers extending out to 1.5 times the IQR.
